Greek Yogurt

Description

Yogurt is a product obtained from the fermentation of milk. Greek yogurt is a type of yogurt that we get when we filter out the whey from the milk, leaving us with a soft and creamy yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams natural yogurt
  • 1 liter skim milk

Steps

  1. Pour the milk in a pot and heat it to 85 ÂºC
  2. Once the temp. is reached, pour the milk and the natural yogurt onto a jar and stir briefly
  3. Close the jar tightly and save it somewhere to keep its temperature
  4. Leave it overnight, preferrably 10 to 12 hours, to let it ferment
  5. After time has passed, use a fabric to filter the whey out for a minimum of 3 hours. The time depends on the texture desired.
  6. Once filtered, the yogurt is now ready. Enjoy!
