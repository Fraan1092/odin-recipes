Milanesa Napolitana

Description

A milanesa is a chicken breast covered in bread crumbs. We make it napolitana by adding tomato sauce, ham and cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 gr bread crumbs
  • 100 ml tomato sauce
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 100 gr mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into thin slices
  2. Pour the eggs in a bowl and beat them to mix the white and the yolk
  3. Dip the chicken slices in the eggs, and then cover them in the bread crumbs completely
  4. Put the milanesas on a pan and into a preheated oven for 10 minutes
  5. After that, flip the milanesas and cook them for another 5 minutes
  6. Then, pour the sauce on top of the milanesas and add a slice of ham and mozzarella cheese
  7. Cook them for 5 more minutes, and they are ready to go!
